East,Wast,South Village

The arsenal moved from QingDao all the way to Taiwan in 1948. They stayed in the warehouse built by the Japanese. The south village was built first. As the population grow in 1951, the east and west village were built.

44 East Village was built in June 1949. Located at where Keelung Road Sector 1, KuangFu South Road, XinYi Road Sector 4 and Keelung Road Sector 1 Land 364 now. On the west side of the arsenal lies the West village, its population are mostly officers and families.

East Village's building is the same as an ordinary military village. Unlike the east village, the south village consists of officers, militants and technicians. The east village is the first village to reconstruct in 1980. 1983, the citizen resident named "ZhongTuo" is built, and got its name by the 44 arsenal logo which shaped like an camel.

44 South Village is located at where XinYi Road Sector 5, Keelung Road Sector 2, SongPing Road and ZhuangJing Road now. The 44 South Village consists of lower-ranked employees.
