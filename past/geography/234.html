|Governing District|

Starting from 1970, Taipei City government started pushing XinYi district development proposal. Before the proposal, the lands are either farmland or 44 arsenal and military use territory. As the arsenal moved and military village rebuild, the place starts to get a more modern view.

|XinYi development zone|

District wise, this place belongs to SongShang district ShihJing community. 1973, the population grow increased the number of the community and was changed to south village community. Mar 12 1990, XinYi District was separated from SongShan district and it belongs to JingXing community. November 1999, 44 South village's location merge with the nearby park named JingXing and is the place where the XinYi resident recreation center.
