44 elementary school

44 Elementary School, right beside the south village, provides a place for kids in the village to study. The school is roughly one hectare. It was a single-floor wooden structure. First row us the classroom, second row is the offices. The main entrance is located on the old XinYi Road sector 5. There were a news office near the gate not long ago. Entering the school reveals a wall which represent the spirit fortress, and a field not more than 150 meters. The center of the field has a flag pole. The classroom was structured in unique shapes. On the left side of the field there's a artwork classroom, right beside is an airstrike shelter. On the right side of the field there's a P.E. class equipment room.

The east side of the school is the single arsenal employee resident. The south side is farmland. North reveals the 44 nursery right across the street. The north of the nursery is the arsenal itself, the size of the nursery is the same as the elementary school. It contains three buildings. Early school employee and school expenses are paid by the arsenal. Some of the employee are also family member of the military village.
