Food

For the people who used to live in the military village, the food stands for the home they came from. It wasn't luxurious or expensive, but it shows the purity of the traditional culture. Now the residents moved into the city and a lot of the village were reconstructed into new buildings, the preservation of the original taste of the culture has become lesser and lesser. Making the food extremely unique.

Military village is the creation of the era, it consists of different part of the Chinese culture. The government retreat to Taiwan in 1949, taking the major 8 Chinese food together with them, which later become the unique food culture in military village. They usually use whatever they have in their home and create the maximum difference between each dish. Every family has its own unique style.

Having flour-made food as mainstream, making it popular in Taiwan. Other than the home-made dishes, oven rolls, fried bread stick, there's a unique snack called "QiZhi". It was made by mixing flour and sugar, fried and cut into small square-shaped snacks. These traditional food in military village isn't difficult to make, the most special part is that it uses the existing ingredient in home and make the most out of it.
