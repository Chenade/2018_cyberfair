44 South village renovation proposal

44 south village's architecture preservation proposal is built upon the idea of not only preserving the original, but also allows the community's resident, teachers and students to create a special bond to the history by making this place the recreation center of this community. The building integrity and stability became the fundamental part in this project. In 2001, Taipei City government City Development department announced the 44 south village preserve zone and public space renovation proposal. It includes these major points:

1 Preserve the history of the city, maintain the street and space of the village to its original state. For example, the bone-shaped narrow street and the living room , bedroom are all in its original shape. Hosting guidance events to let the citizens understand more of the city they live in.
2. Make 44 south village a open space for the XinYi development zone. South village and elementary school don't have walls to separate them, making the school a place where the residents can interact with each other. By making some the building the school history room, students can learn more about the history of their school.
3. Public arts and road junctions are redesigned to represent more of the memories of the military village.
Other than preserving what's left, new public arts are created to bind with the memories of the resident, creating new memories.

Repairs
According to the plan by Taipei City government, the parts preserved are being repaired to become a new public space for citizens to have recreation activities. The main target of the repair is durability. 44 south village become a historical building, its value came from the buildings history and culture. The repair is done by not changing the look of it, but the materials have been replace by more durable parts. It's repairs include the roof, outer walls, internal structure, doors, windows, stairs, rain shield and the old equipment inside.
